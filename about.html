Chase Weckler is a 22-year-old charter captain with deep roots in the waters of Door County, Wisconsin. Born and raised along the peninsula’s rugged shores, Chase grew up with a fishing rod practically in his hands — catching perch off the dock before he could even tie his own shoes. His love for the water and the outdoors was nurtured by generations of local fishermen, and by the time he was a teenager, Chase was already leading friends and family on successful fishing trips across Lake Michigan and Green Bay. Known for his easygoing personality and sharp instincts on the water, Chase has turned a lifelong passion into a career, offering unforgettable fishing adventures to both seasoned anglers and first-timers alike.

As the owner and captain of Chasin' Charters, Chase prides himself on providing a personal, authentic experience rooted in the traditions of his hometown. Whether it’s reeling in trophy walleye, chasing salmon runs, or simply sharing stories about life on the lake, he brings an infectious enthusiasm and deep local knowledge to every outing. With a genuine respect for the environment and a strong sense of community, Chase Weckler represents the next generation of Door County captains — hardworking, welcoming, and forever tied to the waters that raised him.
